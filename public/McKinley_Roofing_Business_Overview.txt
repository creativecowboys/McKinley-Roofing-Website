EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
================================================================================

McKinley Roofing and Restoration is a family-owned, locally operated roofing 
contractor serving Douglasville and the broader West Georgia region. The 
company has established itself as a trusted partner for residential roofing 
solutions, specializing in roof repair, replacement, and storm damage 
restoration. With a strong commitment to honest work, quality craftsmanship, 
and exceptional customer service, McKinley Roofing has built a reputation for 
exceeding customer expectations while maintaining deep community ties.

The company operates under a 'Quality over Quantity' philosophy, prioritizing 
thorough, reliable service over volume. As an Owens Corning Preferred 
Contractor, McKinley Roofing meets strict industry standards and offers both 
manufacturer and labor warranties to ensure customer peace of mind. The 
business also specializes in working with insurance companies to help 
homeowners maximize their claims and minimize out-of-pocket expenses.

CORE SERVICES
================================================================================

1. ROOF REPAIR SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
McKinley Roofing provides comprehensive repair services designed to address 
damage quickly and effectively, preventing minor issues from becoming major 
problems. The repair service is particularly valuable for homeowners dealing 
with storm damage, aging roofs, or sudden leaks that threaten their property.

Repair Services Include:
• Thorough inspection to assess the full extent of damage
• Prompt and efficient repairs using high-quality materials
• Professional leak detection and repair
• Shingle replacement for damaged or missing shingles
• Flashing repair and replacement around chimneys, vents, and valleys
• Emergency roof repair services for urgent situations

The repair process begins with a detailed inspection to identify all issues, 
not just the obvious ones. This comprehensive approach ensures that underlying 
problems are addressed, preventing callbacks and ensuring lasting results. 
Emergency services are available for situations requiring immediate attention, 
such as active leaks during storms.

2. ROOF REPLACEMENT SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
When repairs are no longer cost-effective or when a roof has reached the end 
of its useful life, McKinley Roofing provides complete replacement services 
for both residential and commercial properties. The replacement process is 
carefully managed to minimize disruption while ensuring superior results.

Replacement Process Features:
• Comprehensive inspection to assess existing roof condition
• Customized replacement plan tailored to specific needs and budget
• High-quality roofing materials from trusted manufacturers
• Professional installation by skilled, experienced craftsmen
• Meticulous attention to detail for durability and longevity
• Both manufacturer and labor warranties for peace of mind

The company works closely with homeowners to select appropriate materials that 
balance aesthetic preferences, performance requirements, and budget 
constraints. Installation crews are experienced in proper techniques that 
ensure the roof will perform well for decades to come.

3. STORM DAMAGE RESTORATION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
McKinley Roofing specializes in helping homeowners recover from storm damage, 
including hail damage, wind damage, and other weather-related issues. The 
company has extensive experience working with insurance companies to ensure 
homeowners receive the maximum benefits they're entitled to under their 
policies.

Storm Damage Services:
• Detailed damage assessment and documentation
• Direct coordination with insurance companies
• Assistance with claims process and paperwork
• Expert testimony on damage extent when needed
• Complete restoration to pre-damage condition or better

Many customers have praised the company's ability to work with insurance 
providers, with testimonials highlighting how McKinley Roofing helped them 
receive full roof replacements when they initially thought only minor repairs 
would be covered. This expertise in insurance claims significantly reduces the 
financial burden on homeowners after storm events.

4. PROACTIVE ROOF MAINTENANCE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Preventive maintenance services help homeowners protect their roofing 
investment and extend the lifespan of their roof. Regular maintenance can 
identify and address small issues before they develop into expensive repairs.

Maintenance Services Include:
• Regular inspections to identify potential issues early
• Cleaning gutters and downspouts to prevent water buildup
• Repairing minor issues before they escalate
• Applying protective coatings to extend roof life
• Expert advice on roof care and maintenance best practices

5. GUTTER INSTALLATION SERVICES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Proper drainage is critical for protecting both the roof and the foundation of 
a home. McKinley Roofing installs seamless gutter systems designed to 
effectively channel water away from the structure.

Gutter Services Include:
• Customized gutter design for each property's specifications
• High-quality, durable gutter materials
• Professional installation by experienced technicians
• Downspout installation and strategic water redirection
• Maintenance tips to keep systems functioning optimally

6. INTERIOR AND EXTERIOR PAINTING
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Expanding beyond roofing, McKinley also offers comprehensive painting services 
for both interior and exterior applications, allowing customers to address 
multiple home improvement needs with a single trusted contractor.

Painting Services Include:
• Interior painting for walls, ceilings, trim, and more
• Exterior painting for siding, trim, doors, and windows
• Complete surface preparation (cleaning, sanding, priming)
• Custom color consultation services
• Professional application techniques for flawless results
